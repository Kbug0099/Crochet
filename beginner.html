US vs UK

Did you know the US and the UK use different terms for their stiches? Make sure to check which type of terms a pattern is using before starting. Here is a chart to help.

US is equalivant to UK
  • sc - single crochet
  • hdc - half double crochet
  • hc - double crochet
  • tr - treble crochet
  • dc - double crochet
  • htr - half treble crochet
  • tr - treble crochet
  • dtr - double treble crochet
